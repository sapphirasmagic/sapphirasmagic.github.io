About Sapphira

Sapphira is a physically disabled, neurodivergent Queer Black femme from Toronto, Canada, and is a multifaceted artist with a celestial twist. Born under the Aquarius sun, fueled by an Aries moon, and adorned with a Virgo rising.

Since her youth, Sapphira's heart has always sought to create melodies that speak to her life experiences, crafting songs that hum tales of her true soul's expression. She is currently pouring her spirit into an upcoming EP and a book of poetry.

As a gifted Tarot and Oracle reader, a student of astrology and numerology, and a soon-to-be oracle deck creator, Sapphira blends divination with devotion. Through her readings and creations, she aims to guide others in love, healing, and soul connection.
